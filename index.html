About

An engineer who learned to love the funnel.

I started as a backend engineer building consumer apps — live sports commentary, e-commerce — the kinds of systems where you watch your code touch a million people in a weekend.

Then I moved to product, and what I really fell for was the funnel — the way it tells you the unfiltered truth about your decisions.

These days I split my time between shipping at Times Internet — ET Markets, ET Prime, Times Health+, and a marquee consumer event — and building on the side. Most of my side projects are AI-first consumer experiments I prototype end-to-end.
